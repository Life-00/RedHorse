# Product Overview

This is a React-based mobile web application focused on wellness and fatigue management for shift workers. The app provides:

## Core Features
- **Onboarding Flow**: Multi-step setup for work type, commute time, and wearable device preferences
- **Authentication**: User registration, login, and account confirmation via AWS Cognito
- **Wellness Management**: Caffeine cutoff recommendations, relaxation hub, and wellness tracking
- **Schedule Management**: Shift scheduling and registration functionality
- **Fatigue Risk Assessment**: Daily jumpstart plans and fatigue risk scoring
- **Profile Management**: User preferences and settings

## Target Users
Shift workers (2-shift, 3-shift, fixed night, irregular schedules) who need help managing fatigue and wellness around their work schedules.

## Key User Flows
1. Onboarding → Authentication → Home Dashboard
2. Schedule registration and management
3. Wellness recommendations and tracking
4. Fatigue risk assessment and planning
